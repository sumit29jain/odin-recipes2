lasagna

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this classic lasagna recipe. Layers of rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel, perfectly cooked pasta sheets, and a blend of savory cheeses come together to create a mouthwatering dish that will satisfy your cravings. Whether you're cooking for a crowd or enjoying a cozy night in, this lasagna is sure to please everyone at the table.

This recipe allows for customization, so feel free to add your favorite ingredients such as ground beef, Italian sausage, or fresh vegetables to make it your own. Serve it alongside a crisp salad and some garlic bread for a complete Italian feast that will have everyone coming back for seconds!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  5. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  6. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  7. Repeat the layers - sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and cheeses.
  8. Top with the remaining marinara sauce and cheeses.
  9. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
