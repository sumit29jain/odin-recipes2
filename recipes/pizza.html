Pizza

Description

Indulge in the flavors of Italy with this mouthwatering pizza recipe. Our homemade pizza features a crispy thin crust topped with rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings. Whether you're a meat lover, a veggie enthusiast, or a fan of classic margherita, this recipe is customizable to suit all taste preferences. Perfect for family dinners, gatherings with friends, or a cozy night in, this pizza is sure to satisfy your cravings. Follow our simple steps and enjoy the satisfaction of creating your own delicious pizza right in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough or homemade pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Optional: grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle olive oil over the toppings.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  10. Serve hot, optionally garnished with grated Parmesan cheese, dried oregano, and crushed red pepper flakes.
